Welcome to the application process for the 61st Annual Southwestern Growers Invitational. Thank you for your continued loyalty to this historic tournament and for supporting our mission. Together, we are making a meaningful local impact across the Coachella Valley, helping our youth grow, succeed, and thrive!

Registration is now open!

New this year: All applications must be completed and submitted online by Friday, May 15. Payment is not required at the time of application and will only be requested once invitations are extended.

To ensure fairness and accuracy, we are unable to reserve places in advance. All participants are required to complete and submit an application. We sincerely appreciate your time and effort in doing so, as it allows us to thoughtfully build the field each year.

Thank you for choosing Boys & Girls Clubs of Coachella Valley as a place to make a difference. We look forward to another incredible tournament together!

Boys & Girls Clubs of Coachella Valley & Southwestern Growers Invitational Committee

Applications:

  • Please click the link below to complete the online application.
  • The same link applies to both players and sponsors.
  • Applicants must have a partner to apply. You may submit your application individually (please include your partner's name) or complete it together.
  • Applications are due by May 15 at 5:00 PM.
  • Invitations will be sent on June 12, along with a link for payment.
  • For questions, please contact Bridget Kerr at BKerr@bgcofcv.org.
