Virtual Grand Auction

Bidding is NOW OPEN!!!

300+ Exciting Auction Items

Trips, Art, Jewelry, Dinners, Sports Memorabilia, Wine, a Yorkie, and More

There are two ways to participate

  1. Mobile Phone

    Text the word Kids to 71760 Using this Text2Bid option provides bidders real time notifications if out bid or after winning an item.

  2. Computer or iPad

    Go to bgckids.org. Using this website option does not provide bidders real time notifications if out bid or after winning an item. Bidders must track their bidding status manually.

Bidders must provide name, phone, email and valid credit card information. Cards will not be charged until after the auction closes and only if bidders won an item or made a cash donation.

Terms and Conditions

The Auction

The Auction opens at 12:01 AM, Friday May 1, 2020 and closes at 11:59 PM Tuesday, May 5, 2020. When the bidding window opens all Donors and Bidders must create an account and provide their credit card information before being assigned a Bidding Number unique to their account.

Bidders can register and bid on their mobile phone, enabling real time notifications regarding items they are actively bidding on. Bidders can also register and bid on other computer/iPad devices, but will not receive notifications; instead they will have to monitor their items manually. Bidders have the opportunity to purchase items without bidding, by selecting the guaranteed price. Winners will be notified after the auction closes at 11:59 PM on Tuesday, May 5, 2020.

After the Auction

Once the auction closes the items will be awarded to the highest bidder, unless the item has been purchased for the guaranteed price prior.

All items are sold as-is. All sales are final. There are no exchanges or refunds on items. However, Boys & Girls Club will refund the full purchase price if there is a failure of performance either on the part of the Boys & Girls Club or the Donor.

California state sales tax will be charged when appropriate. Buyer will be furnished an electronic receipt at the conclusion of the sale.

Certificates can be mailed within 10 days if the winner provides their mailing address to info@bgcofcv.org.
